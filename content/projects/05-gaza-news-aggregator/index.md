An aggregator of all news related to the 2014 military operation launched on [Gaza Strip](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaza_Strip), presented in a descending chronological timeline. Data was pulled from news feeds from [Google News](http://news.google.com/) and [akhbarak.net](http://www.akhbarak.net/).
