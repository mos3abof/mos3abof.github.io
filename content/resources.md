Over time, I have compiled and distilled a list of lists of useful resources.
If you have any feedback please don't hesitate to [send it my way][contact].

If you want to buy me a gift, a book from my [technical book
wishlist][technical-book-wishlist] would be awesome! There is also my
[non-technical book wishlist][non-technical-book-wishlist].

## Database Systems and Reliability Engineering

- High Performance MySQL, by Baron Schwartz, Peter Zaitsev & Vadim Tkachenko.
- [Introduction to Databases][intro-to-databases], by Stanford.
- [CMU 15-721 Advanced Database Systems][cmu-15-721], by Carnegie Mellon
University.

## Linux Internals and Systems Programming

- Understanding the Linux Kernel, 3rd Edition, by Daniel P. Bovet, and Marco
Cesati.
- Advanced Linux Programming, by Mark Mitchell.
- Linux Kernel Development, by Robert Love.
- [The Origins of Linux][origins-of-linux] by Linus Torvalds at the Computer
History Museum.
- [Linux Performance Tools][linux-perf-tools] by Brendan Gregg at Velocity.
- [Give me 15 minutes & I'll change your view of GDB][gdb-talk] by Greg Law at
CppCon 2015.
- [An Introduction to Linux IPC Facilities][linux-ipc] by Michael Kerrisk at
Linux Conference Australia 2013.
- [Beginners Guide to Containers Technology][containers-guide] and how it
actually works, by James Bottomley.
- [CS162: Operating Systems][cs162], by Berkley.
- [Namespaces in Operation (LWN Series)][lwn-namespaces]
- [Control Groups (LWN Series)][lwn-cgroups]

## Distributed Systems

- Designing Data-Intensive Applications, by Martin Kleppmann.
- Cloud Computing Concepts [Part 1][cloud-computing-1], and [Part
2][cloud-computing-2], part of the [Cloud Computing
Scpecialization][cloud-computing-spec], on Coursera, Illinois. _\* Probably
should be called Introduction to Distributed Programming_
- [6.824: Distributed Systems][mit-6-824], by MIT.
- [Distributed Systems Lecture Series][dist-sys-lectures], by Martin Kleppmann.
- [CS436: Distributed Computer Systems][cs436], by University of Waterloo.
- [The TLA+ Video Course][tla-plus], by Leslie Lamport.
- [Distributed Systems Reading Group][dsrg]

## Programming

- Data Structures and Algorithms in Python, by Michael T. Goodrich, Roberto
Tamassia, and Michael H. Goldwasser.
- The Go Programming Language, by Alan A. A. Donovan, and Brian Kernighan.
- [CS107: Programming Paradigms][cs107], by Stanford Center for Professional
Development.
- [6.00.1x: Introduction to Computer Science and Programming Using
Python][mit-6-00-1x], by MIT, on edx.org.
- [Basics of Software Architecture & Design Patterns in Java][sw-arch-java]
\[Not free\], By Holczer Balazs, on Udemy.
- [6.004: Computation Structures][mit-6-004], by Chris Terman from MIT.
- [Computer Architecture][computer-arch], by Carnegie Mellon.
- [Python Path][python-path] \[Not free\], from PluralSight.
- [You Look at it till a Solution Occurs][solution-occurs] by Brandon Rhodes at
PyCon 2017.
- [Python Epiphanies][python-epiphanies] by Stuart Williams.
- [Modern Dictionaries][modern-dicts] by Raymond Hettinger.
- [Transforming Code into Beautiful, Idiomatic Python][idiomatic-python] by
Raymond Hettinger at Next Day Video.
- [Go development with vim-go][vim-go] by Fatih Arslan at DigitalOcean NYC
Meetup.

## Production Engineering and SRE

- Systems Performance: Enterprise and the Cloud, by Brendann Gregg.
- Site Reliability Engineering, edited by Betsy Beyer, Chris Jones, Jennifer
Petoff, and Niall Richard Murphy.
- [6.172: Performance Engineering of Software Systems][mit-6-172], from MIT.
- [Notes from Production Engineering][prod-eng-notes] by Pedro Canahuati at
SREcon15.
- [Production Engineering at Facebook \[Arabic\]][prod-eng-fb] by Ahmed Soliman
at FB Developer Circles & Egyptian Geeks Meetup.
- [Getting Started with SRE][sre-getting-started] by Stephen Thorne at DevOps
Enterprise Summit.
- [Building a Bank with Kubernetes][k8s-bank] by Oliver Beattie at Kubecon.
- [Cloud Capacity Planning][cloud-capacity] by Andrew Park at SouthBay SRE.
- [Getting Started with SRE][sre-getting-started] by Stephen Thorne, from
Google.
- [Scaling Instagram Infrastructure][instagram-infra] by Lisa Guo at QCon 2017.
- [A/B Testing][ab-testing] by Stuart Frisby, principal designer at Booking.com
- [The RED Method How To Instrument Your Services][red-method] by Tom Wilkie.
- [Cloud Performance Root Cause Analysis at Netflix][netflix-perf] by Brendan
Gregg.
